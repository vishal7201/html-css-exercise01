Travel

The 'Queen of Hills' might have lost some of her gleam, but it undoubtedly is one of the most-preferred mountain retreats. Discovered by the weary British, it continued to be a cool getaway for them and the then royalties of India. And why not. Where else can you get stunning views of the Himalayan range, endless trekking options, colonial bungalows exuding old-world charm and picture-perfect picnic spots. Mussoorie itself is perched on a horse-shoe shaped ridge, best explored on foot. The Mall is the hub of all activities and the cool air would reveal your sense of romance! It is home to the icon of Indian writing, Ruskin Bond, who has been living here for close to 45 years. Walk along the Mall, drive down to Dhanaulti..

Food

Mussoorie has quite a few restaurant and eateries that offer a varied range of cuisines. For a quick bite, head out to the Meeting Point Caf√© located on the Mall Road is where you can grab a hot espresso along with some baked treats. The Casa Mia Bakery also located on the Mall Road is the best of the several bakeries in Mussoorie with its wide selection of croissants, cakes and muffins. A popular place for a family meal is the Four Seasons, which is known for its Indian and Chinese cuisine. Whispering Windows located in the Library Bazaar is the place to try out a vast range of multi-cuisine dishes. Most hotels have their in-house restaurants, which provide good meals and a great ambience. The Nabha Palace Restaurant, is an all day dining place .

Relax

There are a large number of hotels and resorts, mostly crowding the area around The Mall. Needless to say, if you're traveling in the peak season, you have to book quite ahead in advance, since these get filled up fast. Among the higher-end hotels you can book the Park Plaza Sylverton Hotel or the Avalon Holiday Resorts. Park Plaza is located conveniently located in close proximity to The Mall and has multi-cuisine restaurants and bar facility. Avalon Resorts is location on the Gun Hill, close to the Mall and offers cottage-like rooms done in style. For budget stay options, you could opt for Hotel Nand Residency, Padmini Niwas and Country Inn. All these have well-done rooms and in-house restaurants serving a range of cuisines.
